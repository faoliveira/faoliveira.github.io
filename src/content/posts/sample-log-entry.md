Testing out the new log layout. Short entries go here — what I'm building, what broke, what I'm figuring out.

This one is mostly a test post to see how the dateline grid renders, check the prose spacing, and make sure the listing page picks up the excerpt correctly.

The Nikki layout is named after the Japanese word for diary (日記). The date column is the dominant element — big day numeral, month and year in mono above and below. Content starts right of the column with a border between them.

On mobile the grid collapses into a single column. The date becomes a horizontal flex row — month, day, year side by side in a smaller monospace treatment.
